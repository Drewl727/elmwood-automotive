Service 02

Paintless Dent Repair

PDR is the preferred method for repairing minor dents, door dings, and hail damage when the paint surface is still intact. It preserves your factory finish and often costs significantly less than traditional body work.

Book an Inspection
  • Door dings and parking lot dents
  • Hail damage repair
  • Minor creases and pressure dents
  • Preserves original factory paint
  • Often completed same-day or next-day
  • No filler, no repainting

Is PDR right for my car?

If the paint isn't cracked or flaking, PDR is usually the best option. Use our damage assessment tool below to find out which service fits your situation.
