Who We Are

Elmwood Automotive LLC

Elmwood Automotive opened its doors in 2013 at 55 Talcott Rd in West Hartford. Sergio Amaral founded the shop with a simple idea: treat every customer the way you'd want your own family to be treated. No inflated estimates, no unnecessary work, no runaround.

Sergio, Tom, and Joe built the shop from the ground up and have stayed focused on the same thing since day one: doing the job right and getting your car back to you on time.

Over the past decade, Elmwood has grown through word of mouth. Our customers come back because we earn it every time. From a door ding to a complete post-collision rebuild, we put the same care into every vehicle that walks through our shop.

We're based in West Hartford and proud of it. This is our community. The people who bring us their cars are our neighbors — and we work like it.
